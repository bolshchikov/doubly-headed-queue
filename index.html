Welcome
to the demonstration page of
doubly-headed queue.

  • Enter a value and click Enqueue head to append it to the head of a queue.
  • Click Dequeue head to remove member from the head of a queue.
  • Enter the value and click Enqueue tail to append it to a tail of a queue.
  • Click Dequeue tail to remove member from the tail of a queue.
