Welcome
to the demonstration page of
doubly-headed queue.

  • Start by clicking Creat to initiate a new queue.

  • Click Enqueue head or Enqueue tail to append a random number to the head or tail of a last queue.

  • Dequeue head or Dequeue tail will remove member from the head or tail of a last queue.

  • Concat allows you to join the last queue to the tail a previous one.
